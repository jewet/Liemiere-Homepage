Crafting Spaces That Tell
Your Story

At Lumière, we believe that the spaces we inhabit shape the way we feel, think, and connect. Founded in 2019, our mission has always been simple: to bring thoughtfully designed, responsibly sourced home furnishings to people who care about how they live. Every piece in our collection is chosen with intention — balancing beauty, function, and longevity.

We work directly with artisan makers across Scandinavia, Japan, and West Africa, ensuring fair trade practices and authentic craftsmanship in every product. Our quality standards are uncompromising: we accept only sustainably harvested timbers, organic textiles, and non-toxic finishes. The result is a home that not only looks exceptional, but feels right — ethically and aesthetically.

Whether you're furnishing your first apartment or refreshing a family home, our expert stylists are available to help you build a cohesive, personalised space. Explore our virtual room planner, browse curated lookbooks, or book a free 30-minute styling consultation online. Great design shouldn't be complicated — and with Lumière, it never is.
